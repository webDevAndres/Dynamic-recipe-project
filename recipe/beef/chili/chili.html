Crockpot Chili

recipe by: Homer J.

The chili recipe uses the finest ingredients found at the supermarket. Once you get a taste of the organic blend of spices and vegetables your tastebuds will be transported to flavor town.

Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

hourglass_empty
Prep time:

25 min

access_time
Cooking time

6 hours

pie_chart
Serving size:

6

  • 2 tbsp. cooking oil
  • 1 cup onion
  • 1 cup chopped peppers
  • 4 tbsp. chili powder
  • 1 tsp. hot chili powder (optional)
  • 1 lb ground beef or chicken
  • 2 cans red beans
  • 2 cans kidney beans
  • 2 cans tomato puree
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream (optional)

Directions

Heat cooking oil in a 2 quart skillet.

Saute onions and peppers for 5 minutes.

Add spices and stir for 30 seconds.

Add ground meat and cook until browned. Approximately 15 minutes.

Pour contents of skillet into 3 quart crock pot.

Rinse beans and place in crockpot.

Open and pour tomato puree and sauce into crockpot.

Cover crockpot and cook on low for 6 hours.

Serve into individual bowls and top with sour cream and cheese, if desired.
